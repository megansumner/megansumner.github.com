Megan Sumner is a copywriter, an editor, and a stick-figurer(er).

She is also an occasional word maker-upper.

After working as a scientist in a hematology research lab for five years, Megan found herself bored with blood. Armed with a playful curiosity, a love for comedy, and the drawing skills of a 4 year old, she said good-bye to the world of beakers. She took a day to rearrange her closet, solve a rubiks cube, build a blanket fort, and play 37 games of Bejeweled Blitz. The next day she started writing copy.
